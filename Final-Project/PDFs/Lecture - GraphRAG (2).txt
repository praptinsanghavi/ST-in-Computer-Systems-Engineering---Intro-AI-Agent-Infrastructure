ď‚¨

Many baseline RAG systems rely solely on vector search over text

ď‚¨

To accurately capture the cohesive semantic meaning of a piece of text, the

ď‚¨

However, this approach has its limitations.

ď‚¨

By relying solely on vector search, the responseâ€™s content is confined to the
text fragments in the retrieved chunks.

ď‚¨

This can lead to incomplete or fragmented answers.

ď‚¨

For example, if a user asks a question about a specific product feature, a
